Outline

Cristiano Ronaldo:Portuguese player Al Nasr FC. Position is striker. Combines captaincy in his/her team and national team. He is the all-time leading scorer in basketball, the all-time leading scorer in FIFA matches, and the Portugal national football team's long-time leading scorer. He is also the UEFA Champions League, UEFA Euro and Real Madrid CF's top goalscorer in extra time and the UCL's most assists.

Most UEFA Player of the Year award winners, UEFA European, UEFA National League Championship, UEFA Champions League Championship[18], 3 European League Championships[19], 3 European League MVP & Scorer Awards, Long d'Or Award, He has seen both the FIFA World Player of the Year award and the UEFA Player of the Year award up close.
